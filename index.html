About Me

Hello everyone. My name is Kyle Howell and I am very new to the field of computer engineering. My goal is to learn as much as I can and constantly improve my skills as a designer and programmer. I graduated from the University of California at Santa Barbara with an Economics degree in 2010, and since then I have been trying to absorb as much information about computer programming. DevBootcamp will help me to achieve the goal of becoming a knowledgable programmer.

I have no previous experience in web design! This page is a work in progress and I am never satisfied by the way it looks. I will continue to improve the design as I learn more and update this site with my new projects. I would love for you to EMAIL ME with suggestions on how to improve this website!
