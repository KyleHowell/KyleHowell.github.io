Happiness and Values

What values come to mind when you are happiest?

Accomplishment Achievement Ambition Challenge Commitment Compassion Confidence Control Cooperation Courage Creativity Decisiveness Enthusiasm Excellence Excitement Faith Freedom Friendship Growth Knowledge Loyalty Responsibility Self-reliance Wisdom

For me, I have been the happiest when my enthusiasm is the highest. I love to be completely focused on one project and devote all of my attention to it. When I find something that I enjoy, whether it be a new activity, or a new book I am happiest when I devote my full attention to it.

I often find struggle when I fail to life up to my values listed about. I especially lose the ambition and enthusiasm when approaching a difficult subject. In general, I would agree what I live up to these values.
